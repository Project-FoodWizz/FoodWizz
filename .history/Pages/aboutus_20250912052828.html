FOODWIZZ History

In 2024, in the vibrant town of La Chorrera, Panama Oeste, a group of 16 young innovators came together with a shared vision: to solve the everyday struggles of small food businesses. They saw how restaurants and food trucks were losing money due to poor inventory management and repetitive tasks. That's when FoodWizz was born - a tool built by youth, driven by purpose, and designed to make a real impact. Today, FoodWizz continues to grow, helping food entrepreneurs take control of their business and focus on what they love most: creating amazing food.
