Never Run Out Again.

FoodWizz is the smart way to keep your inventory under control. No stress, no waste, no surprises. Just fresh products, perfectly organized, and always on hand when you need them.

Graphics

Financial reports and statistics area. Displays key indicators such as sales performance, best-selling dishes, or profit analysis, and supports data export and visualization in dynamic charts.

Alerts

Notification system that alerts the user about low stock of ingredients, products that are about to run out, or critical inventory situations.

Dishes

Section dedicated to recipe management. Here you can define all the dishes on the menu along with their prices and associated ingredients. It allows you to easily view and update your menu.
