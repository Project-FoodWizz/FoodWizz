Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are files stored on your device when you visit FoodWIZZ. Their main purpose is to provide you with faster and more personalized access to our services, based on your browsing behavior and preferences.

Types of cookies we use

According to who manages them:

  • First-party cookies: Directly generated by FoodWIZZ to ensure the website functions properly.
  • Third-party cookies: Used by external services such as analytics or advertising tools.

According to their duration:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when the browser is closed. They help keep your session active.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a defined period to remember your preferences.

According to their purpose:

  • Technical cookies: Allow the basic operation of the site, such as accessing your account or managing inventory.
  • Personalization cookies: Adapt the interface and content according to your language, region, or usage history.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how you use FoodWIZZ to improve our services.
  • Advertising cookies: Display relevant ads, if we run promotional campaigns.
  • Behavioral advertising cookies: Track your browsing habits to show you more relevant content.

Disabling cookies

You can configure your browser to accept or reject cookies. Keep in mind that disabling some of them may affect the operation of certain FoodWIZZ features.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Who uses the cookies?

Some cookies may be managed by third parties such as:

  • Google Analytics: To understand user traffic and behavior.
  • DoubleClick (Google): To display relevant advertising.

Consent

By continuing to browse FoodWIZZ, you accept the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can accept or reject the use of cookies at any time.
