At FoodWizz, we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience when using our website. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time.

How We Use Cookies

FoodWizz uses cookies to:

  • Improve navigation and website performance.
  • Remember user preferences such as language and session information.
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns to optimize the platform.
  • Provide a personalized experience for users.

Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser to accept, reject, or delete cookies at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some functionalities of the platform.

Changes to the Policy

FoodWizz may update this Cookies Policy at any time. We will notify you through the website or application when significant changes are made.
