Accessibility

At FoodWizz, we believe technology should be accessible to everyone. This Accessibility Policy explains the measures we take to make our website and services inclusive, the tools we provide, and how you can reach us if you experience any barriers.

Accessibility Commitment

Our website is designed with the commitment to comply with international accessibility standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1). We strive to ensure that all users, regardless of their abilities, can access clear, functional, and barrier-free information about our inventory management software for food trucks.

Accessibility Features Available

To facilitate navigation and usability, we have implemented the following features:

  • Optimized visual contrast for improved readability.
  • Full keyboard navigation support for users who do not use a mouse.
  • ARIA labels on interactive elements to enhance semantic understanding.

Known Accessibility Issues

We are currently working to improve the experience for mobile users relying on screen readers, especially in the troubleshooting form. If you encounter any difficulties, we encourage you to let us know so we can resolve them as quickly as possible.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website and services to identify new opportunities to improve inclusivity and remove barriers.
